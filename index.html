BHARATNATYAM

Introduction

Bharatnatyam is one of the oldest classical devotional dance form from INDIA. It is regarded as mother of many other Indian classical dance forms, initiated in the Hindu temples. Theoretical base of this form traces back to ‘Natya Shastra’, the ancient Sanskrit Hindu text on the performing arts. A form of excellent footwork and impressive gestures, it includes nrita, nritya and natya.

Kalakshetra Style

Kalakshetra style was developed by Rukmini Devi Arundale. It is one of the most popular styles and is known for its school under the name of Kalakshetra foundation. Most renouned school of Bharatnatyam throughout world.
