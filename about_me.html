SHRADDHA BHONDE-TAILOR

BSc., BPA, MPA - M.S.University

Jouney of dance commenced at age of 11.
Initiated with Diploma in Bharatnatyam along with Bachelors in Science, followed by Bachelors and Masters in Bharatnatyam from M.S.University, INDIA.
Professionally as a performer has won many titles of dance shows, competitions and events.
One of the biggest achievement was representing INDIA in International Folk-Dance Festival in Spain.
Have rich experience of more than 16 years working with several international schools as a dance teacher, choreographer and Performing Arts Coordinator.
Been an official examiner for Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Vidyalaya (National body for DL courses in Performing arts).
As a successful entrepreneur established a school teaching Bharantayam, Folk and Bollywood dance.
Was honoured to be welcomed by City of Winnipeg to conduct classes on behalf of city.
Last but not the least currently taking pride being Bharatnatyam Teacher for Niagara Shiv Mandir (Temple).
